About IFMedical

IFMedical was founded by students, for students. It began with a simple idea: to create a space where premeds could access curated, community-driven resources without barriers.

We're committed to equity, open access, and empowerment. Whether you're studying for the MCAT, navigating shadowing and research, or just starting your journey — IFMedical is here for you.

We believe medicine should be accessible, and so should guidance. Always thinking. Always asking. Always IF.

IF stands for always thinking, questioning, and exploring. That is the goal. If anything on this site violates that purpose, please reach out to any moderators or directly to IFMedical@gmail.com.

Medical stands for access — to education, basic services, and essential resources for everyone who needs them.
