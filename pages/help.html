Help & FAQs

If you're stuck, confused, or just curious — you're in the right place. Browse our frequently asked questions or reach out directly.

Who is IFMedical for?

IFMedical is for everyone — premed, postbac, non-traditional, or just exploring — who wants access to honest, helpful, and no-cost resources.

Can I contribute a guide or resource?

Yes! We love student-generated content. Visit the Get Involved page to learn how to contribute. We also offer the opportunity to submit projects for review and potential publication. We understand how hard it can be to find an outlet to publish your work. IFMedical strives to be an all-encompassing guide to mastering the premedical process.

Is IFMedical free?

Absolutely. This site is 100% student-funded and free to use. No ads, no paywalls. Certain content may be restricted or require payment, but this will only include highly specialized content.

How can I ask a question that’s not here?

Email IFMedical@gmail.com or use the Contact Us form. We're happy to help!
