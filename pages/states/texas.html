🔎 Texas Hub

This page offers a curated collection of Texas-specific resources for gaining clinical experience, volunteering, shadowing, and preparing for both MD and DO medical school applications. At IFMedical, we value both allopathic and osteopathic pathways equally in the journey to becoming a physician.

🏥 Clinical Experience

  • Hospital and clinic volunteer programs (inpatient and outpatient)
  • Public health internships, mobile units, or community clinics
  • County medical internships and government-run medical offices

🩺 Physician Shadowing

  • Reach out to both MDs and DOs in a variety of specialties
  • Use hospital programs to connect with preceptors
  • Explore underserved or rural clinics for unique perspectives

🤝 Community & Non-Clinical Volunteering

  • Support at food banks, youth shelters, and elder care facilities
  • Educational outreach and peer mentorship roles
  • Mental health and crisis intervention services (virtual or on-site)

🏫 Texas Medical Schools (MD & DO)

  • MD: University of Texas – Phoenix
  • MD: Mayo Clinic Alix School of Medicine – Scottsdale
  • MD: Creighton University School of Medicine – Phoenix Campus
  • MD: University of Texas – Tucson
  • DO: A.T. Still University – School of Osteopathic Medicine in Texas (Mesa)
  • DO: Midwestern University – Texas College of Osteopathic Medicine (Glendale)
