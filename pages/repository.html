Resource Hub

This hub collects all downloadable resources from IFMedical — including study guides, planning templates, curated PDF toolkits, and external links. Use them freely, and feel free to share!

  • MCAT Study Templates: PDF and Google Doc versions of daily, weekly, and full-schedule plans.
  • Premed Planner: A printable roadmap for courses, clinical hours, shadowing, and applications.
  • Scholarship List (2025): Curated list of premed scholarships and essay-based awards.
  • State-by-State Opportunities: Directories of shadowing, volunteering, and pipeline programs.
  • PDF Library: Visual mnemonics, content maps, and AAMC-aligned cheat sheets.

To request an upload or submit your own resource, email IFMedical@gmail.com.
