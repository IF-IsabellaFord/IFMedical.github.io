Meet IFMedical

IFMedical is a student-led initiative founded by Isabella Ford, a premed navigating the challenges of medical school preparation. It started as a personal project to document resources, tips, and encouragement for others on a similar path.

We believe that access to opportunity, clarity in planning, and encouragement from real students can make the premed process more humane and hopeful.

Our Mission

  • Transparency: Break down the myths of medical school admissions.
  • Equity: Share resources with students from underserved or underrepresented backgrounds.
  • Empowerment: Help students take control of their journey with clarity and confidence.

Why “IF”?

IF stands for always Thinking, Questioning, and Exploring. It’s about always asking, “what if?” That curiosity fuels medicine, and we aim to support it in every student.

Why “Medical”?

Medical stands for access to Education, Basic Services, and Essential Resources for everyone who needs them.

About the Founder

Isabella Ford is a 3x MCAT taker, full-time researcher, and equity advocate with a passion for Indigenous health and student mentorship. Her journey reflects many highs and lows — and IFMedical was born from those lived lessons.
