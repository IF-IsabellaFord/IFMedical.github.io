🔎 Arizona Hub

Explore opportunities in Arizona for clinical experience, community service, shadowing, and preparing to apply to medical school — all in one place.

🏥 Clinical Experience

  • Hospital volunteer programs across major health systems
  • Opportunities in outpatient clinics and urgent care settings
  • Public health or government internships (e.g., medical examiner, county services)

🩺 Physician Shadowing

  • Reach out to physicians in family medicine, surgery, or internal medicine
  • Use volunteer program connections to request shadowing
  • Consider tribal and rural health centers for unique access

🤝 Community & Non-Clinical Volunteering

  • Food banks, shelter support, and senior care facilities
  • Health education or mentorship programs
  • Crisis response or mental health hotlines (virtual or in-person)

🏫 Arizona Medical Schools

  • University of Arizona – Phoenix
  • Mayo Clinic Alix School of Medicine – Scottsdale
  • Creighton University – Phoenix Campus
  • University of Arizona – Tucson
