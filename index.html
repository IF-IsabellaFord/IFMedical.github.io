Welcome to IFMedical

IFMedical is a student-created survival guide for premeds navigating the path to medical school. Created by Isabella Ford — a 3x MCAT taker, researcher, and advocate — this space is for anyone who’s ever felt overwhelmed but still determined to become a physician.

💡 What You’ll Find Here

  • MCAT Resources: Study timelines, video guides, and success stories.
  • US Hub: State-by-state shadowing, volunteering, and med school data.
  • Get Involved: Share your story, submit opportunities, or volunteer with IFMedical.
