USA Hub

This section provides state-by-state directories of shadowing opportunities, clinical volunteer programs, premed advising contacts, and medical school data. Use the information to explore what’s available where you live — or where you want to go.

  • Arizona: Mayo Clinic, Banner Health, Native Health, U of A College of Medicine–Phoenix
  • California: Scribe America, Kaiser Permanente, UC medical school stats, community clinics
  • New York: Mount Sinai, Columbia summer programs, NYC mentorship initiatives
  • Illinois: Rush Medical, University of Chicago, Chicago Youth Programs
  • Texas: Houston hospitals, Baylor pipeline programs, Dell Med volunteer services

If you have a state-specific opportunity to add, please email IFMedical@gmail.com.
