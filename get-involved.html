Get Involved with IFMedical

IFMedical is always growing — and we believe in building this community together. There are many ways to get involved:

  • Share Your Story: Submit your premed journey or advice for publication on our Blog.
  • Volunteer: Help us review resources, update the site, or mentor other students virtually.
  • Submit a Resource: Know of a shadowing, scholarship, or research opportunity? Send it in here.
  • Join Our Team: We're looking for contributors to help expand content on MCAT, research, and other important topics.

Partner With Us

If you’re a physician, nonprofit, student group, or educational organization — let’s work together to expand access and opportunity. Email IFMedical@gmail.com to get started.
