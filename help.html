Help & FAQs

If you're stuck, confused, or just curious — you're in the right place. Browse our frequently asked questions or reach out directly.

Who is IFMedical for?

IFMedical is for everyone — premed, postbac, non-traditional, or just exploring — who wants access to honest, helpful, and no-cost resources.

Can I contribute a guide or resource?

Yes! We love student-generated content. Visit the Get Involved page to learn how to contribute. We also offer the opprotunity to submit projects for review and potental publication. We understand how hard it can be to find an outlit to publish your work. IFMedical strives to be an all encoupasing guide to master the Premedical Process

Is IFMedical free?

Absolutely. This site is 100% student-funded and free to use. No ads, no paywalls. Certain content may be restricted or require payment, yet this will only include highly specilized content

How can I ask a question that’s not here?

Email IFMedical@gmail.com or use the Contact Us form. We're happy to help!

IF stands for always thinking, questioning, and exploring. That is the goal. If anything on this site violates that purpose, please reach out to any moderators or directly to IFMedical@gmail.com.

Medical stands for access — to education, basic services, and essential resources for everyone who needs them.
